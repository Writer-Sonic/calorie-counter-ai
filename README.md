# Calorie Counter AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 April 2026

## Introduction

Calorie Counter AI ("we", "our", "us") is designed to help users track meals, calories, macros, and related wellness information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and explaining clearly how information is handled when you use our app.

This Privacy Policy describes what information may be collected, how it is used, how it may be stored, and the choices available to you.

## Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect and process the following categories of information:

### 1. Account Information
If you sign in or create an account, we may process information such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Account identifier
- Subscription status

### 2. Health and Fitness Information
If you choose to use nutrition tracking or connect health features, we may process information such as:
- Meals you log
- Food photos you upload
- Barcode scan results
- Calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat totals
- Weight entries
- Goal settings
- Dietary preferences
- Apple Health data you choose to share with the app

### 3. User Content
We may process content you provide in the app, including:
- Meal photos
- Nutrition coach messages
- Voice inputs you choose to transcribe
- Notes, edits, and corrections you make to logged meals

### 4. Device and Usage Information
We may process limited technical information needed to operate and improve the app, such as:
- Device type
- Operating system version
- App version
- Feature usage
- Crash and diagnostic information

## How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide core app functionality
- Save and sync your meals, progress, and settings
- Generate food analysis and nutrition-related responses
- Personalize your experience based on your goals and preferences
- Support Apple Health integrations you choose to enable
- Process subscriptions and restore purchases
- Maintain app security, performance, and reliability
- Provide customer support

## AI and Food Analysis

If you use AI-powered features such as meal scan, nutrition guidance, food analysis, or transcription, relevant content you submit may be processed by third-party AI or backend service providers strictly to provide those features.

These features are intended for general wellness and informational purposes only. They are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, the app may read and write health-related data you explicitly authorize through Apple Health permissions.

Apple Health data is used only to provide and improve app features for you. We do not use Apple Health data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

## Data Storage

Your information may be stored locally on your device and, where needed to provide app features such as syncing, account access, AI processing, subscriptions, and data recovery, may also be stored securely using third-party infrastructure and backend service providers.

## Third-Party Services

We may rely on third-party services to help operate the app, including services for:
- Account authentication
- Cloud storage and syncing
- AI processing
- Subscription and purchase management
- Diagnostics and crash reporting
- Customer support communications

We do not describe these providers by name in this policy, but they only receive the information reasonably necessary to perform their services.

## Advertising

Calorie Counter AI does not use third-party advertising networks to display ads inside the app.

## In-App Purchases

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app product, payment information is processed by Apple and related purchase infrastructure. We do not directly store your full payment card information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the app, process subscriptions, provide AI features, store your data, maintain security, and support app functionality.

## Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you choose to delete your data through the app, we will process deletion in accordance with the app's functionality and applicable requirements.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices

You can choose whether to:
- Create an account
- Connect Apple Health
- Enable notifications
- Use AI-powered scanning, transcription, and coach features
- Delete your data from within the app

You may also manage app permissions through your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

Calorie Counter AI is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us using the Support option in the app.

By using Calorie Counter AI, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.
